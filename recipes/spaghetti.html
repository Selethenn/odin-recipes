Homemade Spaghetti Recipe

The Dish

Spaghetti is a tried and true meal that can almost never go wrong!

This recipe is a family secret and is incredibly simple to make. With just some basic ingredients you can have a healthy and hearty spaghetti ready in no time!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup - Flour
  • 2 - Eggs
  • 1 Cup - Milk
  • 2 Tbsp - Butter
  • 4 - Tomatoes
  • 1/2 lbs. - Ground Beef
  • Salt (to taste)
  • 3 Cups - Chicken Stock

Steps

  1. In a medium sized mixing bowl beat the eggs and flour together.
  2. Slowly add the milk and keep mixing.
  3. Once firm, roll the mixture out on a counter and create your spaghetti.
  4. In a pan over medium heat, let the butter melt and brown the ground beef.
  5. In a sauce pot over medium heat, bring the chicken stock to a simmer.
  6. Add tomoatoes and ground beef to the simmering pot. Let simmer for ~2 hours or until stock is reduced. Mix gently every 15 minutes.
  7. Boil pasta, strain, and add your sauce!
