Eggs on Toast Recipe

The Dish

Eggs on toast is one of the best breakfasts you can wake up to!

It is incredibly easy to make, is healthy, and starts your day off eggsactly how you would want it!

Ingredients

  • 1 slice - Bread
  • 1 - Egg
  • 1 - Avocado (optional)
  • 1 Tbsp - Butter
  • Salt (to taste)

Steps

  1. Toast the bread on a high setting in your toaster.
  2. Over medium heat in a non-stick pan, crack your egg and let cook for 4 minutes.
    • Cook longer for less runny egg
  3. While the egg is cooking butter your toast.
    • Optionally, cut and add your avocado on the toast.
  4. Add your egg atop the toast and season with salt and pepper.
