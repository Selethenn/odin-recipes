Greek Salad

The Dish

Greek salad is a healthy and easy to make salad to spruce up any meal!

Using fresh ingredients you can make an easy salad in just a few minutes to get some great nutrients for your body!

Ingredients

  • 1 - Cucumber
  • 2 - Tomatoes
  • 1/2 - Red Onion
  • 1/2 Cup - Feta Cheese
  • 4 Tbps - Olive Oil
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black Pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Dice the cucumber, tomato, and onion.
  2. Add the vegtables, salt, pepper, and olive oil to a medium sized mixing bowl.
  3. Gently mix or toss the salad.
  4. Serve and garnish with feta cheese over top.
